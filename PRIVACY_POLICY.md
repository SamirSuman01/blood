## 3. Information Collection

### 3.1 Information You Provide

When you register as a blood donor, you may provide:
- **Personal Information:** Name, age, gender, weight
- **Contact Information:** Phone number, email address, physical address
- **Medical Information:** Blood type, last donation date
- **Consent Information:** Health declarations, notification preferences

### 3.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use this platform, we may collect:
- **Device Information:** Browser type, operating system, device type
- **Usage Information:** Pages visited, features used, search queries
- **Location Information:** GPS coordinates (only if you explicitly grant permission)
- **Technical Information:** IP address, timestamps, error logs

### 3.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect payment information (this is a free service)
- We do NOT collect health records beyond blood type
- We do NOT sell or rent your personal information
- We do NOT share your data with third parties for marketing

## 4. How We Use Your Information

### 4.1 Primary Uses

Your information is used to:
- **Connect donors with recipients:** Match blood type requests with available donors
- **Send emergency alerts:** Notify you of urgent blood needs in your area
- **Provide certificates:** Generate donation certificates and track donation history
- **Improve the service:** Analyze usage patterns to enhance the platform

### 4.2 Communications

We may contact you to:
- Send emergency SOS blood requests (if you've opted in)
- Send reminders about donation eligibility
- Notify you about blood donation camps
- Provide important service updates

**You can opt out of non-emergency communications at any time.**

## 6. Data Sharing

### 6.1 Who We Share With

Your data may be shared with:
- **Blood banks and hospitals:** When you respond to SOS requests

- **Emergency services:** In life-threatening emergencies

### 6.2 Who We DON'T Share With

We do NOT share your data with:
- Marketing companies
- Data brokers
- Social media platforms
- Third-party advertisers
- Government agencies (except as required by law)

### 6.3 Legal Disclosures

We may disclose information if:
- Required by law or legal process
- Necessary to protect life in an emergency
- Needed to enforce our terms of service
- Required to prevent fraud or illegal activity
